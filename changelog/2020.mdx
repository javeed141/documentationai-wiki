# 2020 Data Release Log

<Update label="2020-01" description="New Year Kickoff" tags={["new sources", "schema changes", "fixes"]}>
  Few schema changes or major fixes. Hierarchical product data is being prepared with major changes coming soon.

  **Record Counts:** Business 93.5M (+2.2M) · People 11.7M (+1M) · Product 185.6M (+3.4M) · Property 70.8M (+10.1M)

  **New Sources:** 3 Business · 2 Property

  **Schema Changes:**
  - Added `secondaryCategories` field — new pipelines in-progress to populate it

  **Fixes:**
  - Removed various SDS (safety data sheet) data from the `features` field
  - Updated 3 Property, 1 Product sources
</Update>

<Update label="2020-02" description="Erroneous Data Cleanup" tags={["schema changes", "new sources", "fixes"]}>
  Large quantity of erroneous web-crawled data removed, mostly from one particular source.

  **Record Counts:** Business 97.7M (+4.2M) · People 11.7M (unchanged) · Product 156.3M (−29.3M) · Property 86.4M (+15.6M)

  **New Sources:** 2 Business · 2 Product · 1 Property

  **Schema Changes:**
  - `asins` field is now a single value
  - Legacy fields `crawlResultFiles` and `automatedCollection` finally removed
  - Additional `paymentTypes` and acceptable `hours` values added

  **Fixes:**
  - ~40 million Product (and some Business) records removed due to erroneous crawl data
  - Postal code collection accuracy improved
  - Updated 1 Business, 3 Product sources
</Update>
