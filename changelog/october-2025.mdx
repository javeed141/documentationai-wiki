## Property Data: New Counties Source Added

We've expanded our property data sources with new county databases:

- **California** — Fresno County, Sonoma County
- **Indiana** — Lake County
- **Iowa** — Polk County
- **Nevada** — Washoe County
- **New Jersey** — Camden County

## Portal: County Autocomplete

Added autocomplete functionality to every county text box within the Datafiniti portal for faster and more accurate data entry.
