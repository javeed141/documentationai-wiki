# 2024 Data Release Log

<Update label="2024-02" description="DR8 — Data Quality & Address Fixes" tags={["fixes", "new sources"]}>
  Datafiniti project release 8 is live, focused on data quality improvements and bug fixes.

  **Record Counts:** Business 133.2M (+572K new) · People 30.3M (+13.3M new) · Product 526.9M (+7.9M new) · Property 232.1M (+10.5M new)

  **Property Data Quality Improvements:**
  - Fixed address over-merging of brokers and people at specific building/rental unit addresses
  - Standardized "Street" address prefixes across all data
  - Most recent broker emails and phone numbers now accurately reflected

  **Property Bug Fixes:**
  - Resolved commercial properties merging with unrelated property records
  - Normalized city name "Saint Louis" to "St. Louis"

  **Property Source Updates:**
  - Updated Redfin, Kobo, Crexi, Zumper, Jefferson County tax records, Cityfeet, ForRent, Loopnet, Weichert, Showcase, Rightmove.co.uk

  **Product Source Updates:**
  - Updated Bloomingdales, Target.com, Saksoff5th, Wayfair
</Update>
