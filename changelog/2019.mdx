<Update label="2019-11" description="Merging Logic Overhaul & Multi-Month Completions" tags={["schema changes", "new sources", "fixes", "completed"]}>
  Completed many multi-month schema updates. Biggest change: overhauled key generation and merging logic across data sources.

  **Record Counts:** Business 89.3M (−1.9M) · People 10.7M (unchanged) · Product 173.2M (+12.3M) · Property 36.9M (+9.8M)

  **New Sources:** 2 Business · 1 Property

  **Schema Changes:**
  - Merging logic overhauled with field weighting — certain fields override others to reduce invalid merges
  - **Completed:** `crawlResultFiles` → `crawlIDs` transition
  - **Completed:** `geoLocation` for Business and Property data
  - **Completed:** `dateSeen` single-value transition
  - **Completed:** UPC/EAN/`gtins` field rollout

  **Fixes:**
  - **Completed:** Bad seller ranking data removal from categories and features
  - Fixed ASIN records to use lowercase key values
  - Updated 4 Property, 2 Product, 1 Business sources
</Update>

<Update label="2019-10" description="Database Tooling Overhaul Begins" tags={["schema changes", "new sources", "fixes"]}>
  Database change tooling overhaul began — future schema changes and fixes will roll out faster.

  **Record Counts:** Business 91.2M (−1.1M) · People 10.7M (+1.2M) · Product 160.9M (+20M) · Property 36.9M (+2.1M)

  **New Sources:** 4 Business

  **Schema Changes:**
  - Added `naics` data to People data
  - `mostRecentStatus` now source-dependent — individual domains provide status instead of us deriving it
  - `gtins` check digit verification added to validate global product identifiers
  - In-progress: `crawlResultFiles` → `crawlIDs`, `geoLocation`, `dateSeen`, UPC/EAN/`gtins` continue

  **Fixes:**
  - Bad seller ranking data removal still in-progress
  - Address normalization to USPS values ongoing
  - `mostRecentStatusDate` now collected from sources instead of derived from crawl timestamps
  - Updated 6 Property, 2 Product, 1 Business sources
</Update>

<Update label="2019-08" description="Schema Revisions" tags={["schema changes", "new sources", "fixes"]}>
  Focused on schema revisions and ensuring conversions ran smoothly.

  **Record Counts:** Business 90.1M (+3.1M) · People 9.5M (+0.1M) · Product 129.3M (+5.2M) · Property 32.2M (+7.2M)

  **New Sources:** 3 Business · 1 Product

  **Schema Changes:**
  - Added Product fields `upca`, `upce`, `ean13`, `ean8`, and `gtins` for better product search. Original `upc` and `ean` fields supported until deprecated.
  - `dateSeen` in nested JSON objects now set to single latest-date value (will be renamed to `dateUpdated`)
  - Added `geoLocation` field to Business and Property data combining lat/long for future distance queries

  **Fixes:**
  - Bad seller ranking data polluting categories and features — removal in-progress
  - Removed "Details about" prefix appearing in Product name field
  - Updated 1 Business, 4 Property, 2 Product sources
</Update>
