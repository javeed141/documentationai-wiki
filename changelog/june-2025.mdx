## New People and Property Data Integration

New `people_key` sub-fields linking to corresponding records:

- `people.people_key` — Retrieve detailed homeowner contact information
- `brokers.people_key` — Access license numbers, contact details, and listing history

## New Property Data Fields

- `brokers.firstName`
- `brokers.lastName`

## New People Data Fields

- `propertyKeys`
- `propertiesOwned`
- `propertiesRepresented`

## New Business Data Sources

- New York — data.ny.gov
- Connecticut — data.ct.gov
