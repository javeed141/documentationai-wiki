## Our 2026 Operating Priorities

**Reliability first.** We're committed to keeping our infrastructure running smoothly day in and day out—so your workflows stay uninterrupted and dependable at scale.

**Data quality you can trust.** Accuracy, freshness, and consistency remain non-negotiable. We continue to invest heavily in processes and tooling that ensure our data remains high-quality and up to date.

**Designing for direct value.** Our customer-facing tools are guided by a simple principle: make them intuitive, actionable, and fast to value.
