## Property Data: New "type" Field in Prices

Each price entry now includes a `type` sub-field with values:

- **Sale List** — for sale list price
- **Sold** — price at which the property was sold
- **Rental List** — rental list price

## Improved Field Names

New descriptive fields for clarity:

- `mostRecentSaleListPriceAmount` & `mostRecentSaleListPriceDate`
- `mostRecentRentalListPriceAmount` & `mostRecentRentalListPriceDate`
- `mostRecentSoldPriceAmount` & `mostRecentSoldPriceDate`

## Deprecation Timeline

The legacy fields `isSale`, `isSold`, and `mostRecentPriceAmount` will be fully deprecated by January 31st, 2026.

## Portal Changes

- **Visual Renew Date** — Now shows your exact date of plan renewal
- **County Autocomplete** — Added autocomplete functionality to property data filter
