<Update label="2025-11" description="Price Type Restructure & Legacy Deprecation" tags={["schema changes", "feature"]}>

  **Record Counts:** Property 284.2M (+27.4M) ôñ Business 135.3M (+1.1M) ôñ People 135.4M (+35.8M) ôñ Product 565.3M (+20.6M)

  - `mostRecentSoldPriceAmount` & `mostRecentSoldPriceDate`

  **Migration Notes:**
  - Existing records being updated to new `price.type` structure over next month
  - Backward compatibility available via custom views ã contact Customer Success Manager
  - Legacy fields `isSale`, `isSold`, `mostRecentPriceAmount` fully deprecated by Jan 31, 2026

  **Portal Changes:**
  - "Renew date" added to billing subscription page showing exact plan renewal date
  - County autocomplete added to property data filters

  **Coming Soon:**
