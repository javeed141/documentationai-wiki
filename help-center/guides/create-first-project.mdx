## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login">
    Go to `https://app.dafiniti-javeed.com` and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Project">
    Click the "New Project" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details">
    Add a name and description for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and View">
    Click "Create". Your project appears on the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
