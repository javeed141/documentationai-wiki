## Steps

1. Log in to your dafiniti-javeed dashboard.
2. Click on "New Project" at the top right.
3. Enter your project name and description.
4. Select the relevant template (optional).
5. Click "Create" to finish.

<Callout kind="info">You can edit project settings any time from the Projects page.</Callout>
