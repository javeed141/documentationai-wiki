title: products_keysSourceURLs
---

The `products_keysSourceURLs` view is useful when you just need the `sourceURLs` in your data set.  It also outputs the `keys` and `id` field, just in case.


## Included Fields

| Field | Included Sub-fields |
| --- | --- |
| `id` | N/A |
| `keys` | N/A |
| `sourceURLs` | N/A |
