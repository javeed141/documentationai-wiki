## Property Data Quality Improvements

- **Address Over-merging** — Fixed over-merging of brokers and people at specific building/rental unit addresses
- **Standardized Address Prefixes** — "Street" prefixes are now consistent across all addresses
- **Broker Contact Data** — Most recent broker email and phone data now accurately reflected

## Bug Fixes

- **Commercial Property Merging** — Resolved commercial properties merging with unrelated records
- **City Name Normalization** — Standardized "Saint Louis" to "St. Louis"
