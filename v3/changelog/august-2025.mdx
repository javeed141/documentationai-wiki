## Property Data: Clearer Rental Statuses

New improvements to how rental statuses are tracked:

- New top-level `rentalStatuses` field
- Refined tracking via `mostRecentRentalStatus`, `mostRecentRentalStatusDate`, and `mostRecentRentalStatusFirstDateSeen`

## New County Coverage

Expanded coverage to include **Dane County, Wisconsin**.

## People Data: New Listing Price Fields

- `minListingPrice`
- `maxListingPrice`

## Business Data: New State Source

- Rhode Island — business.sos.ri.gov
