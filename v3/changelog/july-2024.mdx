## New Property Data Sources

Added multiple new property data sources resulting in over 1 million new properties with greater coverage of daily market trends and analytical data.

## New Realtor License Types

Added 13 new state realtor license types for querying broker information:

- Connecticut (CTREC), Florida (FREC), Kentucky (KREAB), Louisiana (LREC)
- Utah (UTRED), Oklahoma (OREC), New York (NYDOS), Arkansas (AREC)
- Nevada (NVRED), Kansas (KREC), Ohio (ODREPL)
- West Virginia (WVREC), Maine (MEREC)

## New Field: `mostRecentRentalPriceAmount`

A new top-level field separates rental prices from other status prices, providing easy access to the most recent rental price without parsing the full `prices` field.

## Streamlined Sign-Up

New sign-up flow lets you create and select free, monthly, or annual plans directly at [portal.datafiniti.co/sign-up](https://portal.datafiniti.co/sign-up).
