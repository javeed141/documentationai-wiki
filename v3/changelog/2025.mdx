# 2025 Data Release Log

<Update label="November 2025" description="Property Pricing Updates" tags={["New Feature","Deprecation"]}>
  ### Property Data: New "type" Field in Prices

  Each price entry now includes a `type` sub-field with values:

  - **Sale List** ‚Äî for sale list price

  - **Sold** ‚Äî price at which the property was sold

  - **Rental List** ‚Äî rental list price

  ### Improved Field Names

  New descriptive fields for clarity:

  - `mostRecentSaleListPriceAmount` & `mostRecentSaleListPriceDate`

  - `mostRecentRentalListPriceAmount` & `mostRecentRentalListPriceDate`

  - `mostRecentSoldPriceAmount` & `mostRecentSoldPriceDate`

  ### Deprecation Timeline

  The legacy fields `isSale`, `isSold`, and `mostRecentPriceAmount` will be fully deprecated by **January 31st, 2026**. Backward compatibility available via custom views ‚Äî contact your Customer Success Manager.

  ### Portal Changes

  - **Visual Renew Date** ‚Äî Now shows your exact date of plan renewal

  - **County Autocomplete** ‚Äî Added autocomplete functionality to property data filter
</Update>
