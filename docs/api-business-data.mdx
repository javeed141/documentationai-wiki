title: Business Data
---

Datafiniti's Business Data API provides access to a comprehensive database of business information, including details about businesses, such as company name, industry, location, contact information, and more. This API enables users to retrieve and analyze business data for various purposes, such as market research, lead generation, and competitor analysis.

## Capabilities:

* Access to detailed business information, such as company name, industry, size, revenue, and contact details.
* Geolocation data, allowing users to map businesses and analyze their locations.
* Financial data, including revenue estimates, growth rates, and financial health metrics.
* Company hierarchy data, indicating parent companies and subsidiaries.
* Advanced search and filtering options, making it easy to find specific businesses or segments of the market.
